Bread and Butter sandwich

Let's learn how to make a Bread and Butter sandwich

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Bread
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread Butter on one side
  3. Spread more Butter on the other side.
  4. Then sprinkle Powdered sugar on both sides.
  5. Stick the two slices together and enjoy!
